Omelettes

Description

An omelette is a quick and delicious dish, perfect for breakfast or lunch. To prepare it, beat three eggs with two tablespoons of milk, season with salt and pepper to taste. Melt some butter or olive oil in a pan, pour in the egg mixture, and cook until the edges start to set. Add your favorite fillings - cheese, ham, vegetables, etc. - on one half of the omelette, then fold it in half. Cook until it's nicely golden. Serve it hot and savor this tasty omelette with a salad or bread for a simple and comforting meal.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons of milk
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or olive oil
  • onions
  • grated cheese

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and beat them using a fork or whisk until well combined.
  2. Add the milk to the beaten eggs and mix again. The milk will make the omelette more fluffy and tender.
  3. Season the egg mixture with a pinch of salt and pepper according to your taste preference.
  4. Prepare your choice of fillings by cutting them into small pieces so they distribute evenly in the omelette.
  5. In a non-stick frying pan, melt the butter or heat the olive oil over medium heat.
  6. Pour the egg mixture into the hot pan and let it cook for a few moments until the edges start to set.
  7. Add your selected fillings on one half of the omelette.
  8. Using a spatula, carefully fold the other half of the omelette over the fillings to cover them.
  9. Allow the omelette to cook for a little longer until it becomes nicely golden on both sides.
  10. Gently slide the omelette onto a plate and serve it immediately.
